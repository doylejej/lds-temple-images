Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the LDS Temple Screensaver Roku channel ("the App") handles information.

Information We Collect

The LDS Temple Screensaver does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information of any kind. The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details.

How the App Works

The App displays photographs of temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints as a screensaver on your Roku device. Images are loaded from publicly hosted URLs. No usage data, viewing history, or device information is collected or sent anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Temple photographs are served from GitHub (github.com). GitHub may log standard web server access logs (IP address, timestamp, file requested) as part of normal server operation. This is governed by GitHub's Privacy Policy. The App developer does not have access to these logs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children. It is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
